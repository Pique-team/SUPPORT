## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How does Pique work?**
A: Pique shows you people within 50 meters of your current location. Send a wave to connect, and if they wave back, you can start chatting!

**Q: Why can't I see anyone nearby?**
A: Make sure you've enabled location services for Pique in your device settings. You also need to be within 50 meters of another Pique user.

### Privacy & Safety

**Q: Who can see my profile?**
A: Only users within 50 meters of your current location can see your profile.

**Q: How do I report someone?**
A: Tap on their profile and select "Report User". We review all reports and take appropriate action.

**Q: How do I block someone?**
A: Go to their profile or chat and tap "Block". They will no longer be able to contact you or see your profile.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app keeps crashing**
A: Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, please email us with your device model and iOS version.

**Q: I'm not receiving notifications**
A: Check that notifications are enabled for Pique in your device Settings > Notifications.

**Q: My location isn't updating**
A: Make sure location services are enabled and set to "While Using the App" for Pique.
